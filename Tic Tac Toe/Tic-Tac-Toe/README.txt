Description:
~~~~~~~~~~
This is a simple Tic-Tac-Toe (Also called X and Zeros) game by AGZ Game Studios
A.K.A Aswin Ganesh. Use mouse click to play.

The first person to click is Player one, and he/she will use X.
The next person will be Player two, and he/she will use Y.
